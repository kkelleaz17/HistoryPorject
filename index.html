US Involvement In WW2

By Kris and Manav

Then Vs Now

Japan and America's relationship was not very good during the time of WW2. They had a ton of problems from past events. Japan was worried that The US would leave them out because The US had done the same thing before. That is why Japan then proceeded to attack Pearl Harbor which only worsened their relationship with the US since the US decided to retaliate after this attack which Japan didn't expect. Japan was worried that the US would start taking supplies from Asia which would impact Japan since they wouldn't have enough resources for themselves. The US also started putting Japanese Americans in camps because they thought they would do an inside operation. This whole mishap caused a ton of problems between the US and Japan which is why their relationship was worsening. Now Japan and the US have a good relationship where they constantly keep up with arrangements and the citizens also visit so Japanese people come to the US and the American people go to Japan. They also share similar goals now and similar values.
