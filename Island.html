Island Hopping

This strategy was very costly for the Japanese and the United States, some key battles include the battles of Guadalcanal, Iwo Jima, and Okinawa. Most of the battles were the United States trying to capture Islands and push forward. Important Battles

  • Battle of Okinawa

  • April to June 1945
  • Okinawa was the largest attacks of the Pacific War, The japanese were using kamikaze attacks and US was just pushing through, this resulted in 50,000+ American casualty and 100,000+ Japanese casualties, this was also a key victory because this island allowed america to invade japanese home land.

  • Battle of Midway

  • June 4, 1942
  • This attack was an Japanese attempt to gain back lost land and gain control of Midway so they could attack the west coast and regain other land lost to the Americans, this was their biggest counter attack during the pacific war.

  • Battle of Guadalcanal

  • August 7, 1942
  • Was one of the greatest battles of the war, The United states won the battle for this island which resulted in the United states gaining a big airfield, this island was between multiple other islands, making airfare cheaper and more effective for the war.

  • Battle of Iwo Jima

  • February 19, 1945
  • The result of the island battles was very big win for the United States because of the airfields and positions the island held, this also caused the US to have over 26,000 American casualties.
